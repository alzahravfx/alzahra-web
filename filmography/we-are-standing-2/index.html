WE ARE STANDING 2

Director :

Release date:October 10, 2010

This is VFX Demoreel for “We are standing 2” short film is based on real event that happened on July 3, 1988, Iran Air Flight 655 (IR655) was shot down by USS Vincennes on the Bandar Abbas-Dubai rout. ALZAHRA VFX ANIMATION STUDIO team created more than 200 heavy vfx & Full CG shots for this short film. We came up with a new technique to create water dynamic for storm and also creating smoke for different shots. Too many modelling, more than 25 people worked on this project, we had very heavy shots like helicopter and ships explosions for this project.
